Pure Javascipt FITS viewer

What is FITS?

Try it now

  • Load a local file
  • Click Header to display FITS header
  • Choose desired contrast algorithm
  • Center left is magnifying glass under cursor
  • Lower left is overall stats and histogram with contrast control
  • Two regions of interest controls on the right, each with
    • On/off (blue is off initially)
    • Contrast
    • Histogram and stats
    • Move by dragging top center
    • Resize by dragging any edge
